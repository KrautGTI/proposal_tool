How Your Solar System Will Work

1When the sun is shining your solar panels capture the free energy.
2Your solar panels absorb the energy and produce direct current (DC) electricity.
3The DC electricity flows to your inverter and is converted to alternating current (AC) electricity to power your home.
4When your system produces more solar electricity than you use, the excess is sent to the grid to help offset the cost of grid electricity used at night or on cloudy days.
