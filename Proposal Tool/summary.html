Rebates

Manufacturer, municipal or utility rebates may be available in your area. Visit the Database of State Incentives for Renewables & Efficiency (DSIRE) for more information.

Solar Tax Credit

A taxpayer may claim a credit of 30% of qualified expenditures for a system that serves a dwelling unit located in the United States that is owned and used as a residence by the taxpayer. Visit the Department of Energy Renewable Energy Tax Credit for more information.

Solar Tax Credit Pay Down

Enables users to calculate the impact of using the solar tax credit amount to pay down the HERO financed amount and reamortize the payment schedule. Assumed pay down date is May 15 the following year. Assumed pay down amount is 30% of total system cost (minimum $2,500).
